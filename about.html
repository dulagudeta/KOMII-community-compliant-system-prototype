About KOMII

Connecting communities with their local government to resolve issues efficiently

Our Mission

KOMII (Community Issues Management and Improvement Initiative) was created to bridge the gap between residents and local government departments. Our platform makes it easy to report, track, and resolve community issues in a transparent and efficient manner.

By streamlining communication and providing real-time updates, we help create cleaner, safer, and better-maintained communities for everyone.

How It Works

1. Report an Issue

Residents can easily report issues like potholes, illegal dumping, or broken streetlights through our simple form.

2. Automatic Routing

The system automatically routes each complaint to the appropriate department for quick resolution.

3. Track Progress

Residents can track the status of their complaints and receive updates until the issue is resolved.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use KOMII?

KOMII is available to all residents within our service area. You can submit complaints as a guest or create an account to track your submissions.

What types of issues can I report?

You can report various community issues including road damage, sanitation problems, public safety concerns, and more. Our system will route your complaint to the appropriate department.

How long does it take to resolve issues?

Resolution times vary depending on the issue type and complexity. Most simple issues are resolved within 3-5 business days, while more complex problems may take longer. You'll receive updates throughout the process.

Is there a mobile app available?

Currently KOMII is web-based and works on all mobile browsers. We're developing a dedicated mobile app that will be available soon.
